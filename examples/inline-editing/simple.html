The purpose of this consultative webpage is to facilitate better communication among Chinese people living, working, and studying in Nordic countries. It provides an interactive platform as a service for Chinese communities across the five Nordic countries by standardizing variants of proper nouns, predominantly place names, topographic features, personal names, and commercial establishments.
